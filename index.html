Welcome to our YCET

Younus College of Engineering and Technology (YCET), is a premier institution in Kerala that offers a holistic educational experience. YCET is dedicated to empowering students to become competent professionals and responsible global citizens. With a focus on innovation, research, and industry collaboration, YCET prepares students to excel in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

At YCET, academic excellence is at the core of our mission. We offer diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, providing a strong theoretical foundation along with practical applications through hands-on projects and industry internships. Our experienced faculty members guide students on their academic journey, fostering a conducive learning environment. YCET boasts cutting-edge facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and an extensive library. Specialized labs cater to disciplines such as computer science, electronics, mechanical engineering, and civil engineering. Practical training ensures students acquire the necessary skills to meet industry demands.

Research and innovation thrive at YCET. Our research centers and labs provide an environment for groundbreaking research, with collaborations across industries, government organizations, and research institutions. We believe innovation is key to technological advancements and promote entrepreneurship. Industry collaboration is a priority at YCET. Through industrial visits, expert lectures, internships, and recruitment drives, students gain valuable exposure and build a strong professional network. Our partnerships help align our curriculum with industry trends.

YCET emphasizes holistic development, offering a vibrant campus life with extracurricular activities, student clubs, and community service initiatives. Students explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and receive dedicated support services.
